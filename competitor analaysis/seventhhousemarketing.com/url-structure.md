## URL Structure Analysis

### Pattern
- Clean, no trailing slashes
- Simple slugs
- Hyphenated multi-word URLs
- E-commerce functionality (cart)

### Site Architecture
```
/
├── /about
├── /services
│   └── (individual service pages)
├── /portfolio
├── /newsletter
├── /academy
│   └── (course pages)
├── /toolkits
│   └── (template pages)
├── /contact-us
├── /cart
└── /terms-of-service
```

---

## Observations

1. **E-commerce integration** - Cart for selling templates/courses
2. **Educational section** - Academy and Toolkits for passive income
3. **Portfolio page** - Dedicated work showcase
4. **Newsletter page** - Standalone signup page
5. **"Collaborate" vs "Services"** - Creative naming for services dropdown
6. **Dual URL patterns** - /contact-us vs /about (inconsistent hyphenation)

## Recommendations for Creative Conservator

Consider similar expanded structure:
```

├── /about (or /story)
├── /services
│   ├── /brand-strategy
│   ├── /identity-development
│   ├── /content-creation
│   ├── /content-strategy
│   ├── /social-media
│   ├── /paid-advertising
│   ├── /campaigns
│   └── /copywriting
├── /portfolio (or /work)
├── /journal (or /insights)
├── /resources (future: templates, guides)
├── /contact
└── /terms
```

**Key Takeaway:** The Academy/Resources model could be valuable for positioning Creative Conservator as a thought leader and generating passive income. Consider future expansion into educational content.
