# Case Study Section — Wireframe Options

**Placement:** Between `<Marquee />` and "Who We Are" section on the homepage
**Content:** 2 case studies (e.g., Decked Out Dezigns + one more)
**Each card contains:** Client name, industry tag, one-line challenge, key result, image, CTA link
**Palette:** Cream bg (`#F5F0E8`), deep-red text (`#3B0510`), green accents (`#889063`), Libre Caslon Display headings, Inter body

## Summary Comparison

| Option | Layout Style | Visual Impact | Vertical Space | Scalability | Build Complexity |
|--------|-------------|---------------|----------------|-------------|-----------------|
| **A** | Side-by-side cards | Medium | Medium | Good (grid) | Low |
| **B** | Staggered / offset | High | Medium-Large | Moderate | Medium |
| **C** | Alternating L/R rows | High | Large | Excellent | Medium |
| **D** | Overlapping image+card | Very High | Large | Good | Medium-High |
| **E** | Horizontal carousel | High | Small | Excellent | Medium-High |

**Recommendations:**
- **For minimal page height increase:** Option E (carousel)
- **For strongest visual impact:** Option D (overlapping)
- **For easiest future scaling (3+ studies):** Option C or E
- **For simplest build:** Option A (side-by-side)
- **For best match with the site's editorial aesthetic:** Option B (staggered)
