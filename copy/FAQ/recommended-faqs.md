# Recommended FAQ Structure for Creative Conservator

Based on competitor analysis (especially KSM Marketing's comprehensive FAQ) and aligned with The Creative Conservator's pitch deck voice and values.

---

## Brand Voice Guidelines for FAQs

**Tone:** Intentional, clear, confident, refined—not casual or trendy
**Key phrases to incorporate:** "intentional," "preserve," "protect," "evolve with intention," "experience-driven brands," "brand coherence," "partnership not production"

---

## Recommended FAQs

### 1. What types of brands do you work with?

**Answer:**
We partner with experience-driven brands—those who understand that their identity is something to be protected and elevated, not just marketed. Our clients value artistry, intention, and long-term brand coherence over reactive, trend-chasing content. We work best with brands in lifestyle, hospitality, fashion, beauty, and creative industries who are ready to evolve with intention.

---

### 2. What does "conserve" mean in your approach?

**Answer:**
To conserve is to preserve the essence of creative expression—protecting its meaning, artistry, and identity while shaping its evolution with intention. We don't just create content; we protect what makes your brand distinct while helping it grow. This philosophy guides everything we do.

---

### 3. How does your process work?

**Answer:**
Our process follows four intentional stages:

**Stage 01: Discovery** — We discuss your needs, immersing ourselves in your brand's essence, goals, and vision.

**Stage 02: Proposal** — We present a strategic plan tailored specifically to your brand for approval.

**Stage 03: Onboarding** — We establish workflows, access, and the foundation for true partnership.

**Stage 04: Strategize & Execute** — We implement with discipline and intention, protecting brand coherence while driving measurable growth.

---

### 4. What services do you offer?

**Answer:**
We offer seven core services designed to preserve and elevate your brand:

- **Content Creation** — Photography, videography, social media content, and written storytelling
- **Content Strategy** — The intentional framework that defines what you communicate and why
- **Brand Strategy** — Clarifying who your brand is, what it stands for, and how it shows up
- **Identity Development** — Visual elements that define how your brand looks, feels, and communicates
- **Social Media Management** — Ongoing curation and oversight of your digital presence
- **Marketing Campaigns** — Strategic, goal-driven initiatives across targeted platforms
- **Paid Advertising** — Data-driven ad placements on Meta, Google, TikTok, and more

---

### 5. What makes The Creative Conservator different?

**Answer:**
We approach branding as something to be protected, not just promoted. While others focus on trends and reactive content, we provide clear, disciplined, and strategically guided creative work that eliminates reactive content, protects brand coherence, and supports long-term growth. Our role extends beyond delivery—we offer clear guidance, direct feedback, and shared accountability for results. Partnership, not production.

---

### 6. What is Content Strategy vs. Content Creation?

**Answer:**
**Content Strategy** is the intentional framework—the "what," "why," and "how" of your brand's communication. It includes brand analysis, content architecture, platform strategy, creative direction, and editorial planning.

**Content Creation** is the execution—producing the actual photography, videography, social media content, and storytelling that brings your strategy to life.

Strategy ensures every piece of content is purposeful rather than reactive. Creation brings that purpose to life with clarity and artistry.

---

### 7. Do you work with new brands or only established ones?

**Answer:**
We work with both. For emerging brands, we help establish a strong strategic foundation and visual identity from the start. For established brands, we help preserve what's working while guiding intentional evolution—ensuring growth doesn't compromise core identity.

---

### 8. How do you ensure brand coherence across all touchpoints?

**Answer:**
Through strategic frameworks and documentation—playbooks, briefs, and guidelines that ensure consistency over time. Every visual, word, and concept we create exists for a reason and supports a defined objective. We refuse misaligned initiatives and maintain brand integrity through long-term thinking.

---

### 9. What level of involvement is required from me?

**Answer:**
We work collaboratively and strategically with our clients. During Discovery and Onboarding, we'll need your input to understand your brand deeply. Beyond that, we handle execution while keeping you informed at key milestones. Our goal is to free you to focus on your business while we protect and elevate your brand presence.

---

### 10. What's the investment for working together?

**Answer:**
Our partnerships are tailored to each brand's needs and scope. During our Discovery conversation, we'll discuss your goals and provide a clear proposal with transparent pricing. We believe in partnership, not just production—so our pricing reflects the strategic depth and ongoing collaboration we provide.
